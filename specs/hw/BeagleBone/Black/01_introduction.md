# Chapter 1: Introduction

> Source: https://docs.beagle.cc/boards/beaglebone/black/ch01.html

## Introduction

This document serves as the System Reference Manual for the BeagleBone Black, covering its use and design. Throughout the documentation, the board is referred to as "the board" or "BeagleBone Black," with occasional references to the original BeagleBone for comparison purposes.

The design is subject to change without notice as improvements are made based on community feedback and real-world experience. Software updates occur frequently and operate independently of hardware revisions, meaning they do not trigger revision number changes.

Users are encouraged to check the documentation repository regularly for the most current information, available at the BeagleBoard git repository.

## Organization Mission

BeagleBoard.org focuses on democratizing technology through open-source hardware and software. The foundation designs affordable single-board computers for developers, hobbyists, and educators, supported by comprehensive documentation and an active community.
